About

Hi.

I live and work near Manchester, UK.

For work, I’m a Principal Software Engineer at Ivanti (nee LANDESK, nee AppSense) mostly working on shipping v1 products - the three most recent projects have involved; cloud technologies (AWS, Azure), web-UI, web-services, and Windows/Mac agents. I love the dynamic and creative nature when collaborating with a team to birth new v1 products.

Previously at AppSense I was Head of Research; leading a small, skilled team to identify and solve complex problems, and explore next generation markets and technologies. As exciting and free as that role was, shipping real software to real users is what I enjoy most.

From 2010 to 2012 I lived and worked in San Jose, CA, USA. An amazing experience personally and professionally.

I hold a number of UK and US patents.

For non-work, I create and ship apps and services.

Outside of technology, I love spending time with my three young children. And when I’m not doing that I’ll be busy; reading books, doing photography, or playing video games.
